Format Snapshot

League Phase: 36 clubs in one table, 8 matches each, 4 home and 4 away.

Qualification: Top 8 go straight to the Round of 16. Clubs 9–24 enter the playoff round. Clubs 25–36 are out.

Knockouts: Two-legged ties through semifinals. Final is one match at a neutral venue.

Tiebreakers: Aggregate tie → sudden-death overtime periods → best-of-5 shootout.
